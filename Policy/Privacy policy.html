At MatView, protecting your personal information is our top priority. We fully acknowledge that your personal information belongs to you, and we are committed to securely storing and carefully processing the information you share with us.

Collection and Use of Personal Information

We place the highest value on your trust. Therefore, we collect a minimal amount of information, only with your permission, and use it solely for its intended purposes. We do not provide your information to third parties without your knowledge.

Data Protection and Security Measures

MatView is dedicated to ensuring the protection of your data, including:

  • Technical Security: We use Secure Sockets Layer (SSL) software to encrypt the information you input during transmission, especially when entering your credit card or net banking details.

  • Internal Management Procedures: We have strict internal management procedures to protect your data.

  • Physical Data Protection: We employ physical security measures to protect against unauthorized access to our servers and systems.

Continuous Improvement

We constantly re-evaluate our privacy and security policies and adapt them as necessary to address new challenges. We do not and will not sell or rent your personal information to anyone, for any reason, at any time, unless:

  1. In response to a valid legal request by a law enforcement officer or government agency.

  2. With your consent.

  3. For statistical or other representation purposes without disclosing personal data.

Data Storage

The information collected from you will be stored on secure servers and systems. MatView maintains secure and reasonable security practices and procedures, including:

  • Password Protection: Limiting access to specified personnel on a need-to-know basis.

  • Encryption: Using encryption if required and other physical security measures to guard against unauthorized access.

  • Managerial, Technical, Operational, and Physical Security Controls: Measures commensurate with the information assets being protected and the nature of our business.

Access to Information

Access to your registration information and personal financial data is strictly restricted to MatView employees and contractors who need to know this information to operate, develop, or improve the service. These employees or contractors may be subject to discipline, including termination and criminal prosecution, if they fail to meet these obligations.

Protecting Your Anonymity

We strive to allow you to retain the anonymity of your personal identity. You are free to choose a Login ID email address and password that keeps your personal identity anonymous. We only reveal those numbers of your account as required to enable us to access and provide the required services related to your accounts.

Third-Party Access

With your consent, MatView will connect electronically to your online bank, credit card, and other online financial accounts to process your order. We also use third parties, such as courier companies, to deliver products that you order on our website to your delivery address.

Thank you for your continued interest and support. We are committed to providing you with inspiring and fascinating digital experiences while ensuring your personal information is protected.
