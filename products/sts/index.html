Solar Energy Training System:

    Solar Energy Training System is educational tools designed to teach individuals and professionals about photovoltaic (PV) technology, the process of converting sunlight into electricity. These systems simulate real-world solar energy generation and provide hands-on experience with various components and configurations.

      Photovoltaic (PV) training systems are comprehensive educational tools designed to provide hands-on experience and in-depth knowledge of solar PV technology. These systems are invaluable for students, researchers, and professionals seeking to understand the intricacies of designing, operating, and optimizing solar PV systems.




      By simulating real-world scenarios, these systems allow users to delve into the wiring and interconnection of various components within a standalone PV system. A series of carefully curated experiments, divided into three distinct sections, guide users through a progressive learning journey.

These specifications describe a versatile power supply system that includes both fixed DC power supplies (3.3V, 5V, 12V, 48V) and a variable AC voltage supply (0-230V). The system supports precise adjustments in current and voltage, ensuring stable and reliable performance across a range of applications.

  • Solar PV Characteristics: Experiments in this section elucidate the fundamental characteristics of solar PV cells and panels. Users can explore the impact of factors such as tilt angle, radiation intensity, and temperature on energy generation. Additionally, the role of diodes in solar panels is investigated.

  • Standalone PV System: This section delves into the practical aspects of standalone PV systems. Through hands-on experiments, users gain insights into the various components that constitute such systems, their interconnections, and their individual functions.

  • Research Experiments: The research section empowers users to explore advanced topics, such as Maximum Power Point Tracking (MPPT). By leveraging software control and analyzing PV and IV curves, users can delve into the optimization of solar PV systems for maximum efficiency.
  • In essence, PV training and research systems bridge the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical application. They empower individuals with the skills and expertise needed to contribute to the growing field of solar energy.
