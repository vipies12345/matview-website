Power Supply

Power Supply:

    A variable DC power supply is a versatile device used to provide a direct current at various voltage levels to power electronic circuits and devices. This equipment comes with adjustable voltage and current settings, allowing users to precisely control and regulate the output power. It is commonly used in laboratories, workshops, and electronic repair shops for testing and troubleshooting purposes. The variable DC power supply typically consists of a power transformer, rectifier, voltage regulator, and output terminals. It provides a stable and reliable power source, ensuring that the connected electronic components receive the necessary power to function optimally. With its ability to deliver a wide range of voltages, this power supply is an essential tool for professionals working with electrical and electronic equipment.
