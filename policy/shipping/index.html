Shipping Policy

Free Shipping Across India

We are pleased to offer free shipping on all orders across India. Our goal is to ensure that your purchase reaches you promptly and in excellent condition.

Quality and Performance Checks

All our products undergo rigorous quality and performance checks before dispatch. This ensures that only the highest quality products, meeting our strict standards, reach our customers.

Order Processing Time

Orders are typically dispatched within 1–2 working days following successful quality and performance verification. We strive to process and ship your order as swiftly as possible.

Dispatch and Tracking

Once your order has been dispatched, you will receive an email containing tracking details, allowing you to monitor the shipment and estimate the delivery date.

Estimated Delivery Time

Delivery times vary based on your location. Estimated timeframes are as follows:

  • South India: 15–30 days
  • North India: 10–20 days

Delivery Serviceability

Delivery timelines are based on our standard delivery partners and depend on their service areas. In cases where extended delivery time is anticipated, we will notify you at the time of dispatch.

Important Information

  • Address Accuracy: Please ensure your shipping address and contact details are accurate to avoid delays or misdelivery.
  • Business Days: Our working days are Monday to Friday, excluding public holidays. Orders placed during weekends or holidays will be processed on the next working day.
  • Unexpected Delays: While we aim to deliver within estimated timeframes, unforeseen circumstances such as weather, strikes, or transport disruptions may cause delays. We appreciate your understanding.
